StackConnect is the all-in-one mobile companion for app developers. Manage your App Store Connect and Firebase accounts from a single, beautiful native iOS app — with more integrations coming soon.

KEY FEATURES

App Store Connect
• Browse all your apps with search, sort, and favorites
• View and edit version metadata: description, what's new, promotional text, keywords, URLs
• Manage app versions across all platforms (iOS, macOS, tvOS, visionOS)
• Submit for review, cancel review, release, and reject versions
• Edit App Information: bundle ID, SKU, category, content rights, age rating
• Manage app privacy declarations per locale
• Manage accessibility declarations per device family
• Browse customer reviews with infinite scroll, filter by rating
• Reply to reviews and delete responses directly from the app
• View app analytics with daily charts: installs, deletes, downloads, impressions, page views
• Manage TestFlight beta groups and testers
• Add builds to beta groups, invite team members
• Manage team members and roles
• Track app history and review submissions

Firebase
• Browse Firebase projects and apps
• Manage Remote Config parameters and conditions
• View analytics dashboard
• Send Cloud Messaging campaigns with audience targeting

Multi-Account Management
• Add multiple accounts per provider
• Granular permissions per account (apps, versions, users, reviews, TestFlight, analytics)
• Permissions hierarchy: view, edit, delete, add
• Offline-first: load instantly from local cache, sync from API in background
• Cascading deletion: removing an account cleans up all related data

Secure Account Sharing (Team & Lifetime plans)
• Export accounts as encrypted .scexport files
• AES-256-GCM encryption with password protection
• Share access with your team members
• Recipients import the file with the password and gain instant access
• Per-resource permission selection during export

More Integrations Coming Soon
• StackConnect is actively growing — beyond App Store Connect and Firebase, more developer platform integrations are on the way. Stay tuned.

Privacy first: all credentials are stored securely in the iOS Keychain. Account data is cached locally with SwiftData. No analytics, no tracking, no third-party servers — just you and Apple/Google's official APIs.
